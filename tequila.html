OUR HISTORY

A PERSONAL ENCOUNTER WITH TEQUILA

A few years ago, on a trip to Jalisco, Mexico. During a tour between hills and mountains, we had the opportunity to learn about the culture of Tequila.

The conversation with producers who maintain the purest essence of the traditional distillate and their respectful adaptation to practices that care for the environment, made us fall in love with Tequila. We understood why it is the best known Mexican drink in the world.

We are pleased to be able to share with people from anywhere on the planet, the clear and varied nuance of flavors that a carefully prepared drink like Hacienda Los Lobos Tequila provides.

PROCESS

Our Tequilas are 100% pure agave type Weber-Azul Tequilana. On average, 6 kg of agave are used to distill a liter of 100% pure tequila.

WHITE TEQUILA

It is considered the purest because its contact is minimal or null with the wood of the stills. Its transparent color is due to the fact that it is the liquid that is obtained after going through two distillations.


At the end of them, the commercially permitted alcoholic strength is adjusted, and its notes (citrus, fruit or other flavors) are also adjusted according to the aroma and flavor that characterizes us in Tequila Hacienda Los Lobos.

REPOSADO TEQUILA

Our Tequila Reposado offers an intense flavor, faithfully reflecting the taste of the agave elixir aged in white oak barrels.

The origin of our Tequila Reposado comes from blue Weber agave harvested in Jalisco. It presents a golden or pale gold color. In terms of its aroma and flavor, clear fruity and herbal notes stand out, with hints of vanilla. Upon tasting it, its great personality is confirmed; the fruits, herbal notes, and sweet touches like vanilla linger on the palate. It is perfect to enjoy alone or with company.

PREMIUM PLATA TEQUILA

Tequila Plata is a crystalline and vibrant spirit, made from Blue Weber Agave from Jalisco. It has an aroma that blends fruity notes, cooked agave honey, citrus touches, and subtle floral and herbal notes. Aged for 15 to 45 days, it maintains its freshness and herbal flavor, offering a smooth and pure experience that reflects the essence of mature agave.
